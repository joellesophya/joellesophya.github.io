Research

My work is on developing re-sampling methods in the context of association mapping with binary traits in structured samples

  • Quasi-Likelihood Approach

    Generate replicates of the phenotype using a quasi-likelihood framework

  • Parametric Bootstrap

    Use a computationally efficient MCMC-based algorithm to fit a model from which we generate replicates. Also implement a retrospective testing approach

  • Retrospective approach

    Generate genotype replicates from a retrospective model where the distribution of the genotypes is proposed conditioned on the phenotype and covariates (if any)
